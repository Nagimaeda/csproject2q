Mikhail Ganzon

He/Him || 16 || University of the Philippines Integrated School
In my opinion, I think the discriminatory acts need to be more broad, and not limited to only gender discrimination as the bill implies. Furthermore, I hope that this will encourage Filipinos to be more open to foreign ideas, as we are a religious country and there are still some things that the older generation consider as "taboo". All in all though, I appreciate the bill and I would hope for it to be passed, as this will help many closeted people be less ashamed to be who they really are, as I personally know some people like them.
