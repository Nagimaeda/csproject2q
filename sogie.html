What is SOGIE?

SOGIE stands for Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity/Expression.

Sexual Orientation - Refers to the person's identity based on the gender of the person they feel sexual or emotional attraction for

Gender Identity - Refers to how one identifies as; man, woman, neither, fluid, etc. A biological male can identify as a woman. A biological woman can identify as a man.

Gender Expression - Refers to how one expresses their gender identity usually appearance, clothing and behavior; most common classifications are masculine and feminine expression.

Changes After Implementation

After the implementation of the SOGIE Bill, people afraid of expressing their SOGIE might feel safer as society will hopefully become more open-minded and knowledgable about the said topic thanks to the programs to be launched by the government. The summary of this bill, as we see it, is that people who belong to the LGBTQI+ will be protected from the harmful discriminatory behavior exhibited by society.

About the Bill

The bill was filed for the first time by the late Miriam Defensor-Santiago and Representative Etta Rosales. Failing to pass successfully, Defensor-Santiago re-filed the bill to no avail until her last term in 2016. In August of 2019, a controversy regarding the bill sparked following an incident involving transgender woman Gretchen Diez. She was told to leave the female washroom in a mall, after which she filed a complaint, which caused the SOGIE bill to be brought up and into the public eye.
